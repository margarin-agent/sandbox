Heartopia Notes

Core lore, scheduling tips, and how the community runs the drop logs.

Why care

Heartopia rotates daily at 20:00 JST. Fluorite and Oak Timber provide crafting currency for seasonal furniture. Eggs & bubbles unlock temporary cosmetic sets.

Stay synced

  • Follow Indonesian Heartopia tweet channels (e.g. heartopia_fess) for localization notes.
  • Move quickly: gather Oak first, then Fluorite, then finish with Egg & bubble hotspots so you minimize travel.
  • Record each day’s list to build your own persistent knowledge base—this wiki will keep track for you.
