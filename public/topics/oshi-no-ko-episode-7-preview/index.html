Staff credits, story beats, and streaming details for the Feb 25 “Scandal Arc” continuation.

Episode 7 arrives February 25

The next Oshi no Ko episode (Series Episode 31 / Season 3 Episode 7) airs on Wednesday, February 25 at 23:00 JST and is available hours later on Crunchyroll and HIDIVE.

Staff & streaming notes

  • Director: Daisuke Hiramaki (Shirobako Episode 19)
  • Storyboard: Yasuhiro Irie (Sword Art Online Alternative: Gun Gale Online)
  • Script: Jin Tanaka (Love Live! Nijigasaki High School Idol Club)
  • Studio: Doga Kobo continues to animate the third season.
  • Streaming: Crunchyroll (simulcast) and HIDIVE (exclusive season 3 rights) carry the new episode worldwide.

What to watch for

Episode 7 keeps pushing the Scandal Arc forward. Six months after POP IN 2, MEM-Cho’s grind is finally paying off and B-Komachi is on the verge of a major breakthrough. Aqua is juggling a multi‑talented entertainer career, Akane’s acting path is steady, and Kana now hides most of the brightness she once had. Ruby, however, keeps climbing within the industry, using her public image as bait to unearth the truth behind Ai and Goro’s deaths.

The preview synopsis teases a tense agency scene: Kana apologizes after being photographed by a reporter, and when the others ask, “Will you still try to save Kana even if it means pain?” Ruby responds without hesitation, “Of course.” Aqua’s new method to keep Kana safe is hinted at but left as a cliffhanger in the preview stills.

Preview visuals

The official release includes several stills showing Ruby’s determination, Kana’s shaken state, and Aqua’s grim plot to protect her.
