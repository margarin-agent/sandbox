Indie Games February 2026

February 2026 brought a packed month for indie releases, with something for everyone from roguelike enthusiasts to cozy game fans. Steam Next Fest also made its return, offering hundreds of free demos for upcoming titles.

Steam Next Fest February 2026

Dates: February 23 – March 2, 2026

Steam's Next Fest returned as a digital alternative to the E3 trade shows of old, prioritizing demos for up-and-coming indie games. It's a zero-investment opportunity to explore the biggest and most curious hits on the horizon while discovering what excites you as a player.

The event featured:

  • Hundreds of indie game demos
  • Roguelike and roguelite showcases
  • Ghost-hunting games to stylish action-RPGs
  • Opportunities to find new wishlist additions

Featured Releases

Mewgenics

From: The creators of The Binding of Isaac
Release: February 2026
Platform: PC (Steam)

Mewgenics tasks players with breeding the ultimate army of cats. Things get weird, grim, and a little grotesque, but the gameplay promises to be a lot of fun. This tactical roguelite features:

  • Five bosses in the Next Fest demo
  • One difficulty mode
  • 120 gambits to master
  • Roguelike depth with strategic cat breeding

Milestone: Mewgenics hit 1 million copies sold on Steam in February 2026, making a "meowssive splash" in the indie scene.

Lil Gator Game: In the Dark DLC

Release: February 12, 2026
Platforms: All consoles and PC (Steam)

A charming sequel that literally doubles the size of the base game, adding a new area in the caves beneath the island. Building on the foundation laid by A Short Hike, Lil Gator Game tells the story of a little gator on summer vacation who builds a giant LARP-style adventure on a holiday island.

Themes explored:

  • Childhood and coming of age
  • The value of play
  • Adventure and discovery

The DLC expands this wholesome experience with fresh content below the surface.

Battle for Asciion

Release: February 5, 2026
Platform: PC (Steam)

A unique indie title that embraces ASCII aesthetics with a clever twist — you can save screenshots as .txt files. This distinctive feature makes sharing your moments in-game as simple as copying and pasting text.

Kissed By A Star

Release: February 2, 2026
Platform: PC (Steam)
Developer: zDS

An ambitious RPGMaker project from developer zDS, described as "the big game he's always dreamed of." Set in a world where the sun has gone out and civilization has reached a new age, an old evil has emerged.

Features:

  • Turn-based combat with a full party
  • Changing points of view
  • Huge cast of characters
  • Epic narrative scope

Tearscape

Release: February 2, 2026
Platform: PC (Steam)

A 2D action-adventure modeled after Dark Souls, set in a gothic world full of baddies, puzzles, and a mandate to hunt. The world is vast and mimics the challenging combat and atmospheric exploration of its inspiration.

Tagline: "Where tears outstream the rain, the world weeps and calls it balance. You were forged to hunt, and thus, the hunt begins."
