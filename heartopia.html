Pony Series badges & rewards

VG247 and Heartopia.gg both highlight the pre-launch login puzzles that pump out limited-time passes, while the Feb 14–Mar 15 window keeps filling the Tree of Harmony with daily watering.

  • Watering the Tree of Harmony earns Elements of Harmony and charges up milestone rewards—more Exhibition Passes, Badge Pull Tokens, and Moonlight Crystals unlock as the tree strengthens.
  • Badge Pull Tokens buy Pony Series badges; completing each of the three badge sets awards ten more time-limited Exhibition Passes, and finishing all three unlocks the Town Collaboration Series badges.
  • Six Mane 6 gacha pools (one per pony) drop five outfits plus a dedicated vehicle. The no-duplicate/"blind box" badge pulls let you trade extras with friends or swap them for other goodies, so every pull pushes progress forward.
  • Collect every Mane 6 item to unlock the Harmony Car rainbow mount, and keep logging in to claim the Spike pet skin while the pre-launch puzzles are still active.
