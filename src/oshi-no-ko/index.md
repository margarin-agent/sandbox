## Release cadence
Season 3 debuted Jan 14 2026 after an advanced screening of Episode 25 on Dec 28 2025. The run covers **11 weekly episodes** (Sundays 01:00 JST), streaming in simulcast on Crunchyroll and HIDIVE.

## Episode schedule
1. Ep 1 – Jan 14
2. Ep 2 – Jan 21
3. Ep 3 – Jan 28
4. Ep 4 – Feb 4
5. Ep 5 – Feb 11
6. Ep 6 – Feb 18
7. Ep 7 – Feb 25
8. Ep 8 – Mar 3
9. Ep 9 – Mar 10
10. Ep 10 – Mar 17
11. Ep 11 – Mar 24

## Where to watch
- **Crunchyroll:** global simulcast with English subtitles.
- **HIDIVE:** selected territories with English + periodic Spanish subtitles.
Episodes unlock within hours of Japanese broadcast.

## Cast & Music
- Ai Hoshino – Akari Kitō
- Aqua / Ruby – Kaori Maeda / Hinata Satō
- Score: Yugo Kanno (continuing synth-pop/orchestral hybrid opener and insert themes).

## Community notes
Episode 5 (Feb 11) is trending for its deep dive into SUR’s motivations, plus tease for the idol concert arc. Crunchyroll News & fan blogs release behind-the-scenes content each week.
