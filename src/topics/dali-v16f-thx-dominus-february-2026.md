## A 16-Inch Bass Monster

Danish audio manufacturer **DALI** (Danish Audiophile Loudspeaker Industries) has elevated its flagship **SUB V-16 F** subwoofer to the pinnacle of home cinema certification: **THX Certified Dominus** — the highest performance level recognized by THX for large home cinema spaces.

Officially launched at **ISE 2026** (Integrated Systems Europe) in Barcelona, the V-16 F represents DALI's commitment to extreme bass performance without sacrificing fidelity.

## THX Dominus Certification

What does THX Dominus mean?

- **Highest residential THX rating** — Designed for the largest private cinema spaces
- **Cinema-scale performance** — Commercial theater-level output for home environments
- **Stringent testing** — Passed comprehensive THX laboratory measurements for distortion, frequency response, and output
- **Reference quality** — Meets Lucasfilm's standards for accuracy and clarity

As of **February 2026**, the V-16 F holds this elite status, placing it among an exclusive class of home theater subwoofers capable of delivering professional-grade bass.

## Design Highlights

### Custom 16-Inch Driver
The heart of the V-16 F is a purpose-built driver engineered to deliver:
- **Controlled excursion** — CSS suspension ensures linear bass even at extreme levels
- **Low distortion** — Precision motor assembly maintains clarity under pressure
- **Deep bass extension** — Room-shaking low-frequency response

### High-Power DSP Amplification
The 1,500-watt amplifier uses advanced digital signal processing to:
- Optimize driver performance in real-time
- Maintain precision at high output levels
- Adapt to room acoustics for optimal response

### Quadrupole Port System
The innovative port design:
- Reduces port noise and turbulence
- Extends low-frequency output
- Improves efficiency for demanding home theater setups

## Target Audience

This subwoofer is built for:
- **Dedicated home theaters** — Large private cinema rooms
- **Audio enthusiasts** — Those pursuing reference-quality bass
- **Audiophiles with space** — The V-16 F requires significant room volume to perform optimally

### Room Size Requirements
THX Dominus certification means the V-16 F is rated for:
- **Volume**: 3,000+ cubic feet (85+ cubic meters)
- **Seating**: 8+ rows of seats
- **Screen size**: 100+ inch projection or large panel displays

## The DALI Legacy

Founded in **1983** in Denmark, DALI has built a reputation for:
- **In-house driver manufacturing** — Custom designs, not off-the-shelf components
- **Natural sound philosophy** — Balanced, musical presentation across product lines
- **Statement products** — The KORE loudspeaker series represents the company's ultimate expression of audio engineering

The V-16 F continues this tradition, bringing flagship performance to the subwoofer category.
