# Nintendo Switch 2 – February 2026 Games Spotlight

Nintendo Switch 2 continues to dominate with a strong lineup in February 2026. The eShop charts show healthy competition between first-party titles, ports of popular AAA games, and highly anticipated sequels.

## February 22, 2026 – eShop Charts

### Top 10 All Games

1. **Mario Tennis Fever** – Nintendo's latest sports entry holds the #1 spot, fending off strong competition from Resident Evil and Xenoblade titles.
2. **Resident Evil Generation Pack** – Capcom's horror collection makes a strong showing.
3. **Xenoblade Chronicles X: Definitive Edition Upgrade Pack** – The remaster's upgrade pack enters at #3.
4. **Resident Evil Requiem** – New RE title debuts high on the charts.
5. **Xenoblade Chronicles X: Definitive Edition – Nintendo Switch 2 Edition** – The full Switch 2 version launches strong.
6. **Fallout 4** – Bethesda's post-apocalyptic RPG finds a new home on Switch 2.
7. **Cyberpunk 2077** – CD Projekt Red's open-world epic brings next-gen visuals to the handheld.
8. **Pokemon Legends: Z-A – Nintendo Switch 2 Edition** – The latest Pokemon adventure targets Switch 2 with enhanced features.
9. **Resident Evil Requiem Deluxe Edition** – Premium edition of Capcom's horror title.
10. **Donkey Kong Bananza** – A new Donkey Kong title enters the top 10.

### Key Insights

- **Mario Tennis Fever's dominance**: Despite strong releases from Capcom and Xenoblade, Nintendo's sports title maintains the top spot.
- **Resident Evil momentum**: Multiple RE titles charting shows Capcom's commitment to Switch 2.
- **Xenoblade Chronicles X strong start**: Both the upgrade pack and full Switch 2 edition land in the top 5.
- **Pokemon Legends: Z-A**: The next Pokemon entry is already generating significant interest.
- **AAA ports thriving**: Fallout 4, Cyberpunk 2077, and Red Dead Redemption all charting proves Switch 2 can handle demanding games.
