# Spotify Taste Profile and Music Genre Trends March 2026

## Spotify Launches Taste Profile: AI Recommendations Get User Control

**Published:** March 13, 2026 (SXSW announcement)
**Rollout:** Beta to New Zealand Premium subscribers

Spotify has launched Taste Profile, a new AI-powered feature that gives Premium users unprecedented control over their algorithmic recommendations. The feature allows users to see and adjust how the platform interprets their listening habits across music, audiobooks, and podcasts.

### How Taste Profile Works

The feature displays a summary of user listening habits with a "Tell us more" prompt interface. Users can:

- Request more of specific genres they enjoy
- Remove unwanted genres from recommendations
- Submit broader contextual requests such as:
  - "Prioritize my favorite rock bands"
  - "Reduce mainstream pop suggestions"
  - "Suggest more relaxing playlists"
  - Upbeat music for training workouts
  - News podcasts for commutes

Spotify's AI algorithms analyze these instructions and update recommendations accordingly. The feature remains optional—users can choose not to engage and continue using Spotify as usual.

### Strategic Context

Taste Profile arrives as Spotify expands its AI-driven feature suite to retain subscribers and differentiate from competitors. The launch follows Spotify's Prompted Playlist feature, which entered beta in New Zealand in February 2026 before expanding to US and Canadian users. That tool allows specific playlist requests such as songs from particular TV shows.

Co-CEO Gustav Söderström announced Taste Profile at SXSW on March 13, 2026. The beta rollout begins with New Zealand Premium subscribers, continuing Spotify's pattern of testing features in smaller markets before global expansion.

## Top 10 Music Genres on Spotify in 2026

**Published:** March 16, 2026
**Source:** artist.tools Spotify Data Deep Dive

A comprehensive analysis of Spotify streaming data reveals the top 10 music genres dominating the platform in 2026. Each genre operates within its own distinct ecosystem with specific strategies for discovery, playlist penetration, and audience engagement.

### 1. Hip-Hop / Rap

Hip-hop's journey from 1970s New York block parties to global streaming dominance solidifies its position as one of the most powerful genres on Spotify. Characterized by rhythmic spoken-word lyrics over beat-focused instrumentals, the genre offers a rich ecosystem of engaged listeners and high-volume playlisting opportunities.

**Key Success Pattern:** Strategic playlist penetration drives sustained success. Drake's consistent presence on Today's Top Hits and RapCaviar keeps music in constant rotation. Travis Scott's team ensures tracks land on influential playlists upon release.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Identify quality playlists with consistent follower growth and low bot activity
- Optimize music SEO with trending sub-genre keywords ("lo-fi beats," "UK drill," "conscious rap")
- Track placement impact on monthly listeners and stream counts
- Build compelling pitches referencing artists with similar sounds on editorial lists

**Insight:** Hip-hop's diverse sub-genres have dedicated curator communities. Researching these niches is essential for finding your audience.

### 2. Pop

Pop's formula of catchy melodies and broad commercial appeal makes it an undeniable titan, dominating Spotify's largest playlists and capturing the biggest cross-demographic listener base. The genre's versatility allows fusions with nearly any other genre.

**Key Success Pattern:** Sustained playlist placement drives long-term relevance. The Weeknd's presence on Today's Top Hits demonstrates consistent playlist momentum. Olivia Rodrigo's breakthrough accelerated through targeted placements introducing music to massive audiences.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Target authentic playlists showing genuine follower growth
- Avoid damaging placements with high bot activity using bot detection tools
- Define niche with specific sub-genre keywords ("synth-pop," "indie-pop," "pop-rock")
- Gain foothold in smaller playlists (Fresh & Chill, Indie Pop) before aiming for giants

**Insight:** The pop genre is a machine built on momentum. Niche playlist traction creates the initial push needed for major playlist consideration.

### 3. Electronic / EDM

Electronic dance music's evolution from underground club scenes to massive global festivals confirms its place among top genres. The diverse category covers house, techno, dubstep, and more, powered by influential playlists, blogs, and festival tie-ins.

**Key Success Pattern:** Smart playlist targeting and sub-genre understanding drives success. Calvin Harris and Zedd achieve widespread play through pop-oriented dance playlists. ILLENIUM strategically secures placements across future bass and melodic dubstep niches. Deadmau5 demonstrates direct-to-fan approach for dedicated following.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Vet playlists for bot activity—EDM is particularly susceptible to fake streams
- Optimize for sub-genre search with specific keywords ("tech house," "future bass," "lo-fi house")
- Search both general "EDM" and specific sub-genre terms for broader opportunities
- Analyze remix performance to understand audience preferences

**Insight:** The EDM landscape is built on sub-genres with dedicated curators and fanbases. Targeting specific niches like house music is key.

### 4. Rock / Alternative Rock

Rock's legacy ensures its place as a top genre, even as mainstream market share has shifted. From classic rock to modern alternative and indie, the genre cultivates deeply passionate and engaged fanbases with significant playlisting opportunities.

**Key Success Pattern:** Connecting with niche communities drives success. Tame Impala dominates psychedelic rock playlists. The 1975 penetrates major alternative playlists. Arctic Monkeys maintains presence on indie rock lists.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Find niche playlists focusing on alternative and indie rock
- Optimize artist SEO with specific keywords ("garage rock revival," "dream pop," "modern classic rock")
- Analyze peer monthly listener growth to identify effective breaking playlists
- Pitch for editorial features by referencing similar artists on All New Rock or The New Alt

**Insight:** Rock's strength lies in diverse, loyal sub-communities. From shoegaze to post-punk, independent curators power these niches.

### 5. R&B / Soul

R&B's evolution from classic soul roots to a modern, genre-blending force secures its place as a top genre. Defined by smooth production, emotional vocal performances, and lyrical depth, the genre attracts deeply engaged audiences seeking authenticity and artistic substance.

**Key Success Pattern:** Smart artistic positioning and consistent playlist presence builds dedicated followings. SZA crossed from neo-soul to mainstream stardom. Khalid appears regularly on major R&B playlists. The Weeknd fuses R&B with pop aesthetics for massive commercial impact.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Find authentic playlists with high listener engagement
- Target niche keywords ("neo-soul," "alternative R&B," "chill R&B")
- Track performance to understand which songs resonate most with R&B listeners
- Craft compelling pitches explaining fit for specific moods and audiences

**Insight:** Contemporary R&B is incredibly diverse, ranging from ambient to soulful pop fusions. Identifying your specific niche and connecting with key curators is fundamental.

### 6. Indie / Indie Pop

Indie music, defined by artist-driven and independently released ethos, stands as a top genre for authentic artist discovery. Indie pop merges catchy melodies with DIY production, resonating with audiences who value authenticity. The ecosystem powers dedicated curator communities and potent algorithmic playlists.

**Key Success Pattern:** Organic, community-driven growth paves the success path. Clairo's "Pretty Girl" exploded through algorithmic and user-generated playlists. Girl in Red built devoted following by creating music for LGBTQ+ indie community.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Find legitimate playlists with steady follower growth and organic listener activity
- Discover emerging curators before playlists become oversaturated
- Optimize for discovery with indie-pop-specific keywords ("bedroom pop," "dream pop," "sad indie")
- Benchmark growth against comparable indie artists for realistic goals

**Insight:** The indie community values discovery and authenticity above all else. Success is about connecting with curators and listeners who champion genuine, artist-driven music.

### 7. Latin / Reggaeton

Latin music's explosion onto the global stage, powered by reggaeton's infectious energy, makes it one of the most dynamic and fastest-growing genres. Broad appeal from regional Mexican to Latin trap creates a vibrant, highly engaged listener base.

**Key Success Pattern:** Strategic playlisting drives global crossover. Bad Bunny dominates major playlists like Viva Latino and Baila Reggaeton, securing millions of streams on day one. Karol G and J Balvin achieve crossover by landing on both Latin-specific and global pop playlists.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Vet playlists diligently—Latin genre is heavily targeted by bot networks
- Find authentic, regionally focused playlists for markets like Mexico, Colombia, Puerto Rico
- Research sub-genre keywords ("reggaeton," "trap latino," "corridos tumbados")
- Build strong base in specific sub-genres before aiming for broader global playlists

**Insight:** The Latin music landscape is incredibly diverse. Success often comes from building strong bases in specific sub-genres or regions before global expansion.

### 8. Country / Americana

Country and Americana's deep roots in storytelling and acoustic instrumentation foster fiercely loyal audiences. While global streaming numbers are more modest compared to pop or hip-hop, this creates a less saturated market with significant opportunities for specific sub-genres and regions.

**Key Success Pattern:** Focused regional targeting and authentic sub-genre positioning builds sustainable careers. Morgan Wallen demonstrates modern streaming strategy dominating both country-specific and crossover playlists. The Lumineers show crossover folk appeal. Tyler Childers builds authentic, regional growth.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Identify playlists dedicated to niches like "Americana," "outlaw country," "country-pop"
- Research location-specific search terms ("Texas country," "Appalachian folk")
- Benchmark growth against other artists in your specific lane
- Frame music's story with unique sounds and regional influences

**Insight:** The country and Americana audience values authenticity and narrative. Marketing and pitching should reflect genuine stories in your music.

### 9. Pop Punk / Emo

Pop Punk and Emo see powerful resurgence fueled by nostalgia and new generation listeners. Guitar-driven sound blending punk energy with pop melodies and introspective lyrics creates highly passionate fanbases and excellent opportunities for authentic emotional expression.

**Key Success Pattern:** Tapping into both nostalgic sentiment and modern emotional relatability drives success. Olivia Rodrigo brought pop-punk and emo influences to mainstream charts. Machine Gun Kelly's strategic genre shift reinvigorated career. My Chemical Romance experiences massive streaming renaissance.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Find playlists with authentic engagement and loyal listener bases
- Optimize for genre keywords ("emo revival," "pop-punk," "post-hardcore")
- Target niche editorial playlists with compelling pitches referencing similar artists
- Engage fans through social media and share creative process

**Insight:** Pop Punk and Emo communities value genuine connection and emotional honesty. Writing from personal places builds dedicated followings.

### 10. Afrobeats / Amapiano

Afrobeats' surge from West African origins to global force cements its place as a top genre. Vibrant sound fusing African rhythms with R&B, hip-hop, and electronic influences experiences explosive growth alongside South African amapiano. These genres represent rapidly expanding frontiers with burgeoning playlist ecosystems.

**Key Success Pattern:** Strategic playlisting fuels global ascent. Burna Boy and Rema achieve worldwide recognition through consistent placement on major editorial and user-created playlists. CKay's "Love Nwantiti" exploded through TikTok and subsequently dominated Spotify charts.

**Artist Strategies:**
- Vet playlists diligently—rapid growth brings associated fraudulent activity
- Identify legitimate playlists actively growing listenership and breaking new artists
- Target region-specific keywords ("Ghana afrobeats," "amapiano mix 2026," "Nigerian pop")
- Track geographic reach to understand regional appeal and plan promotions
- Target both Afrobeats and amapiano playlists for crossover appeal

**Insight:** Lines between Afrobeats and amapiano blur, creating crossover opportunities. Artists can target playlists in both genres to maximize reach.

## Spotify Global Payouts: Nigerian Artists Growth

**Published:** March 16, 2026
**Source:** Spotify Loud & Clear Report, TechCabal

Nigerian artists earned over ₦60 billion ($43.92 million) in royalties from Spotify in 2025, generated from 30.3 billion total streams. This translates to roughly ₦2 per stream, representing significant growth from ₦58 billion in 2024—a more than 140% revenue increase over two years.

### Payout Disparities

The per-stream value varies dramatically by market due to Spotify's territorial payout model, which adjusts earnings based on regional subscription fees and economic conditions:

- One million streams in Nigeria: ~$300
- One million streams in Sweden: up to $10,000

Disparity stems from subscription pricing:
- Nigeria: ₦1,600 ($1.17) per month
- Sweden: $13.78 per month

While this model keeps streaming affordable for listeners in lower-income regions, it reduces per-stream revenue for artists in those markets.

### Streaming Growth Metrics

- **Total streams (2025):** 30.3 billion
- **Listening hours (2025):** 1.6 billion
- **First-time listener discoveries (2025):** 1.3 billion (26% increase vs 2024)
- **Editorial playlist additions (2025):** Nearly 2,000
- **Global user playlists featuring Nigerian music (2025):** Nearly 320 million
- **Nigeria user playlists (2025):** Over 12 million
- **Playlists created featuring Nigerian artists (2025):** Over 60 million
- **Domestic market share:** Nigerian artists accounted for over 80% of tracks on Spotify Nigeria's Daily Top 50

### Independent Artist Growth

About 58% of all royalties earned by Nigerian artists on Spotify in 2025 went to independent artists or labels. This indicates a large share of revenue flows outside traditional label structures, highlighting the growing role of independent music in Nigeria's economy.
